The IMAGEN dataset

This web site serves the datasets collected from over 2000 adolescents and their parents, and processed by the IMAGEN consortium to study mental health in young Europeans. It includes demographics, neuropsychological assessments, medical questionnaires, MR neuroimaging and genomics. All types of data were acquired for the baseline, only some of them for the follow-up 1 and follow-up 2.
